Our Services

Haulage

We take pride in offering end to end Haulage solutions that are both competitively priced and designed to meet our client specific needs.

Air Freight

Our reliable air freight service for local as well as international clients is designed to their individual needs and often tailored to fit their budget.

Customs Clearance

Doric Logistic is a professional Customs Licensed Agent in Nigeria, offering custom clearing services for imports and exports through Air and Sea Ports.

Chandelling

Doric offers cost-effective and strategic ship chandelling service to the vessels in need. We are a full-scaleship-chandler with a good connection to local businesses and legislative bodies.

Ocean Freight

We offer a full range of import and export ocean freight solutions from any every port on the globe.

Warehousing

Our ever-expanding services include warehousing and trans load, with warehouses strategically located in Nigeria to facilitate just-in-time deliveries for our customers.
